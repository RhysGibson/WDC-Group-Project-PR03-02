Frequently Asked Questions

What does this site do?

This site shows different hotels and prices for certain rooms for that hotel, allowing you to get the most out of your money.

Can I cancel or change my booking after I have made it?

Yes! Any bookings made can be cancelled or changed for free until 24 hours before the booking takes place, when it will cost to change it.

Do you keep the payment information?

We don't, and whats even better is that you are not required to put your credit card number in so there is no chance of being scammed with us!

So theres really no catch?

No there definitely is not! Feel free to use our services as much as you want hassle free.

Are the prices listed really accurate?

We update our prices daily to make sure that you as a customer are getting the best possible pricing for your hotel rooms.
